CARDJumping Jack
Stand up straight with your feet together and your arms at your sides. Jump up and spread your legs wider than shoulder-width apart while simultaneously raising your arms above your head. Keep your knees slightly bent and your core engaged as you jump. Jump again to return to your starting position, lowering your arms back down to your sides.
CARDLateral shuffles
Start by standing with your feet shoulder-width apart and your knees slightly bent. Take a small step to the right with your right foot and follow with your left foot. Quickly bring your right foot back to its original position, followed by your left foot. Repeat this movement to the right for a set number of repetitions or a certain amount of time. Switch directions and shuffle to the left using the same technique.
CARDSquat jumps
Start by standing with your feet shoulder-width apart and your toes pointing forward. Lower your body into a squat position by bending your knees and pushing your hips back, keeping your chest up and your core engaged. From the bottom of the squat position, quickly push through your feet and jump as high as you can, extending your arms above your head. Land softly on the balls of your feet and immediately lower your body back into the squat position to prepare for the next jump.
CARDMountain Climbers
Start in a plank position with your hands directly under your shoulders, your feet hip-width apart, and your body in a straight line. Bring your right knee towards your chest, keeping your left foot in place on the ground. Quickly switch your legs by jumping and bringing your left knee towards your chest while extending your right leg back. Continue alternating legs as quickly as possible, maintaining a straight line from your head to your heels.
CARDBurpees
Begin by standing with your feet shoulder-width apart and your arms at your sides. Drop down into a squat position with your hands on the ground in front of you. Kick your feet back to a plank position while keeping your arms extended. Immediately lower your chest to the ground and perform a push-up. Push yourself back up to the plank position and quickly jump your feet back to the squat position. From the squat position, jump up as high as you can with your arms reaching towards the ceiling. Land softly on the balls of your feet and repeat the movement for the desired number of repetitions or a certain amount of time.
CARDInch Worms
Begin in a standing position with your feet hip-width apart and your arms at your sides. Bend forward at the waist and place your hands on the ground in front of you. Walk your hands forward until you are in a high plank position with your arms extended. Keep your core engaged and walk your feet towards your hands, while keeping your legs as straight as possible. Continue walking your hands forward and repeating steps 3-4 for the desired number of repetitions or a certain amount of time. To finish, walk your hands back towards your feet and slowly roll up to a standing position.
